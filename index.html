How to make a PB&J

Home Sandwich Recipes Blog

Hello! The topic of this post is how to make the best peanut butter and jelly sandwich.

Here are the ingredients you will need:

  • 2 slices of bread, white or wheat will work
  • 2 tbsp of peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp of jelly, any flavor

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread
  2. Spread the peanut butter evenly over one slice of bread. Be sure to spread it all the way to the edges so there are no dry parts.
  3. Repeat the previous step, only this time using the jelly on the second piece of bread.
  4. Lay the jelly bread on top of the peanut butter bread, with the spread sides facing each other.
  5. Cut the sandwich diagnolly. This doesn't change the taste it's just cuter.
